## 🛠️ Refined MVP Structure: "The Core Four"
We’re stripping the fluff. Your MVP shouldn't just "classify" data; it should **eliminate friction**.

### 1. The Gatekeeper (Communication)
* **The AI doesn't just "respond"; it triages.**
* Every tenant text/email goes through a "Need vs. Want" filter. 
* *MVP Action:* Automated troubleshooting guides sent to tenants *before* a ticket is created (e.g., "Check the breaker" for power issues). This kills 20% of tickets instantly.

### 2. The Estimator (Maintenance)
* **The Logic:** Tenant sends a photo $\rightarrow$ Vision AI analyzes damage $\rightarrow$ AI generates a rough "Market Cost Estimate."
* **Why:** Landlords hate being ripped off by vendors. Giving them a price anchor before they call a pro is a massive value add.

### 3. The Matchmaker (Vendor Dispatch)
* **The Logic:** Don't just "suggest" a vendor. **Draft the Work Order.**
* *MVP Action:* One-click "Approve & Send" button that texts the plumber the photo, the address, and the tenant’s contact info.

### 4. The Ledger (Operations)
* **The Logic:** AI monitors the "Money In vs. Money Out."
* *MVP Action:* A simple dashboard showing "Maintenance Spend vs. Monthly Rent" so the landlord sees their ROI in real-time.
