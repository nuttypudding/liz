# Rename "AI" to "Agent" in User-Facing Copy (P1-004)

## Decision

Use "your agent" instead of "AI" or "Liz" in onboarding and user-facing copy.

## Rationale

The product owner prefers "agent" over "AI" or the product name "Liz" in copy directed at landlords. "Agent" reads as more professional and less anthropomorphized than calling the system "Liz." It avoids the potential confusion of a human-named AI while still conveying autonomy and action.

## Consequences

- All onboarding copy and user-facing strings use "your agent" phrasing.
- The product is still named Liz internally and in marketing contexts.
- UI components that display AI-generated content use "agent" labeling.
